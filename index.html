FAQs

FAQs

Answers to common
questions about Trigent AI

What is TrigentAI?

TrigentAI is an all-in-one business intelligence platform powered by three specialized AI agents. It's designed to help businesses optimize costs (Agent BI), save time through automation (Agent AI), and accelerate growth with strategic insights (Agent GX).

Who is TrigentAI for?

TrigentAI is built for small to medium-sized businesses and enterprise teams who want to make data-driven decisions without needing a large, dedicated analytics department. It's ideal for business leaders, managers, and analysts looking to improve efficiency and drive growth.

How is TrigentAI different from traditional BI tools like Power BI or Tableau?

Traditional BI tools are primarily focused on data visualization and reporting. TrigentAI goes beyond that by providing actionable, AI-generated insights and automations. It's a complete solution that not only helps you understand your data but also helps you act on it to directly impact your bottom line.

What kind of integrations does TrigentAI support?

TrigentAI seamlessly integrates with a wide range of your favorite business tools. Our platform can connect to accounting software like QuickBooks, CRM systems like Salesforce, marketing platforms like Mailchimp, and many others to provide a holistic view of your business.

Does TrigentAI replace my existing software?

No, TrigentAI is designed to work with your existing tools. It acts as an intelligence layer on top of your current tech stack, providing insights and automation that your other software cannot.

How much does TrigentAI cost?

TrigentAI offers flexible pricing plans designed to fit businesses of all sizes. You can find detailed information on our Pricing page, and we offer a free trial so you can experience the full power of the platform before you commit.

Can I try TrigentAI before I buy it?

Yes! We offer a free trial so you can explore the platform and see how our three agents can help your business. No credit card is required to get started.

How secure is my data?

Your data's security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data both in transit and at rest. We also implement strict access controls, ensuring that only you and your authorized users can access your information.
